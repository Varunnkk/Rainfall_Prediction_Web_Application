How It Works
Prediction Method: The model was trained on 1995–2023 daily NASA POWER data. For future dates, it uses historical climatology (average seasonal patterns by Day-of-Year) as the feature input, then applies the Random Forest to predict rainfall. The model captures monsoon seasonality through sinusoidal DOY features and learned patterns from 3 decades of data.

200 estimators, learning rate 0.05, max_depth=5. Sequential error correction. Strong on tabular data.
🧠 Neural Network (MLP)
Layers: 128→64→32, ReLU, early stopping. Learns nonlinear patterns but slightly less stable than RF.
